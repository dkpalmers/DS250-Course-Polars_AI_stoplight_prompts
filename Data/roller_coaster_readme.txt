This dataset contains attributes about 635 US roller coasters built between 1915 and 2020 and 										
in operation as of 2020 according to the Roller Coaster Database.										
																	
Data primarily comes from: 										
Roller Coaster Database. 										
Missing data was acquired through other sources such as:										
Ultimate Roller Coaster										
Coasterpedia										
Coaster Grotto										
Wikipedia										
										
Product of InSTEP: Invigorating Statistics and Data Science Teaching through Professional Learning
