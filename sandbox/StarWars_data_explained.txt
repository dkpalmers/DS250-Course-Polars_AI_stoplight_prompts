The star wars data files explained:

StarWarsOriginal:
The original dataset we have used in the past

StarWars_with_emails:
The original starwars dataset with some unique emails generated onto it
There are three email types: Google, Yahoo, and Myorg

StarWars_emails_deconstructed:
This takes the emails from StarWars_with_emails and deconsstructs them.

The alternative files:
These files were the first attempt at creating the emails. The only real difference is that the Myorg
emails don't use the same generation logic. They may not be needed at all.
